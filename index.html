Our Story

A Corner of the City That Smells Like Home

BrookHaven Cafe started in 2017 as a narrow shopfront with a hand-built espresso bar and a menu written in chalk. We had two baristas, one grinder, and a belief that coffee deserved the same attention as fine food.

Seven years later, we still pull every shot by hand, still cup every new origin before it touches your cup, and still sweep the floors ourselves. Some things shouldn't scale.
